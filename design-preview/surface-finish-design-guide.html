Post-Processing DFM Guide

01 / Minimum Wall Thickness

Reinforced Features

Maintain a wall thickness of at least [ 1.2mm ] to survive mechanical tumbling and bead blasting without deformation.

Ultra-thin structures

Features under [ 0.8mm ] are highly susceptible to snapping during vibratory finishing cycles.

02 / Internal Cavity Access

Vented Channels

Add drainage holes with min [ 3.0mm ] diameter to allow vapor or chemical media to circulate freely.

Deep Blind Holes

Vapor polishing cannot reach deep internal surfaces, resulting in inconsistent "Raw" finishes inside the part.
